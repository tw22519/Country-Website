Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is a country in Africa that was founded on April 18th, 1980. The population of this country is 15.99 million people. They have sixteen different languages, including Shona, English, Tshwa, Xhosa, Ndebele, Chewa, Southern Sotho, Tswana, Tsonga, Venda, Tonga, Kalanga, Zimbabwean English, Sena, Zimbabwean Sign Language, and Nambya.

This is their flag.

They are surrounded by other African countries.

Their landscape is very beautiful.
